The Solar System

The Solar System contains eight planets that are gravitationally bound around the Sun, with the Earth as the third closest. Each planet's orbit is within the same plane. Alongside the four terrestrial planets and the four gas giants, there are millions of other rogue objects such as asteroids, comets, and meteors circling the Solar System all at once. Humanity has sent spacecraft as far as Neptune (Voyager 2), but the outer reaches of the Solar System are still largely unexplored.
